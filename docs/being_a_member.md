# Being a member of a nation

This article covers your abilities while being a member of a nation.

## 1. Invites and membership of a nation

### Accepting or Rejecting an invitation

You can see your invitation by running the `/invites` command.

To accept an invitation from a nation run the `/invite_accept` command, to reject run the `/invite_reject` command.
You will have to provide the nations name, as multiple invitations could be pending.

> This will look like this:
>
> * `/invite_accept nation:New Nation`
> * `/invite_reject nation:New Nation`

### Leaving a nation

You can leave a nation by running the `/leave_nation` command.

> This will look like this:
>
> * `/leave_nation nation:New Nation`

## 2. Linking MC Account

You can link you Minecraft account to the bot by running the `/link_minecraft` command. Simply provide your IGN.
This will display the IGN next to you discord username in bot commands.

> This will look like this:
> 
> * `/link_minecraft username:my_account_name`

## 3. Getting infos about nations

### Listing nations

You can get a list of nations by running the `/list_nations` command.

### Displaying a nation

You can get a full display of a nation by running the `/display_nation` command.

> This will look like this:
>
> * `/display_nation nation:New Nation`
